Case Study · Branding & Web Design

Villa Paris
— rebuilt from scratch

A premium hospitality venue in Piešťany had the product. It was missing the brand. We fixed that — identity, website, copy and SEO, all as one integrated system.

The Problem

Great property. Zero presence.

Villa Paris offered genuine value — a peaceful setting in Piešťany, proximity to the spa district and the ADELI Medical Center, comfortable rooms, a family atmosphere. But its visual identity and digital presence didn't communicate any of that.

Potential guests landed on the website and couldn't quickly understand what made it worth booking. The brand felt generic. The copy didn't address the questions people actually ask before reserving. Bookings were being lost before the conversation even started.

Project Scope

Brand Identity Redesign

New visual system — logo, colour palette, typography — built to feel warm, premium and instantly recognisable.

Website Rebuild

Restructured layout prioritising scannability, comprehension and booking conversion from the first scroll.

Hospitality Copywriting

Guest-focused messaging that answered real booking concerns — location, comfort, what to expect on arrival.

Local SEO Strategy

Optimised for searches around Piešťany accommodation, spa proximity and the ADELI Medical Center catchment.

The Approach

Branding and web as one system — not two separate jobs

Most agencies hand off the logo to a designer and the website to a developer. The result is two things that don't quite fit together. We treated this as a single integrated brief.

Brand Direction

Warmth over generic luxury

The visual language was deliberately built to mirror the actual guest experience — welcoming, calm, family-run — rather than copying generic hospitality aesthetics. Warmer tones, softer logo mark, elegant type choices that feel approachable rather than cold.

Web Strategy

Decision-first content architecture

Every section was structured around one question: what does a potential guest need to know before they decide to book? The layout eliminates friction — key information surfaces immediately, CTAs appear at natural decision points.

Copywriting

Copy that converts, not just describes

Hospitality copy fails when it lists features without context. We wrote around outcomes — what the stay actually feels like, why the location matters, what guests with specific needs (spa visits, medical centre proximity) can expect.

What Was Delivered

A complete brand and digital foundation

Full logo system — primary mark, variations, usage guidelines
Colour palette with accessibility ratios defined
Typography system — heading and body type pairing
Full website redesign — all pages, mobile-first
Conversion-focused copy across every page
Local SEO structure — pages optimised for Piešťany accommodation searches
Technical SEO baseline — meta structure, schema markup, sitemap
